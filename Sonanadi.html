Sonanadi Zone: An Oasis of Natural Beauty

The Sonanadi Zone, a serene region within Jim Corbett National Park, is located in the Nainital and Pauri Garhwal districts of Uttarakhand. Its name, derived from the Sonanadi River ("River of Gold"), hints at its pristine natural wealth. This sanctuary offers lush forests, clear rivers, and abundant wildlife, ideal for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Sonanadi Zone offers several Forest Rest Houses (FRHs) for an authentic jungle stay:

  • Halduparao FRH: Inside the sanctuary, two rooms, cook available, self-provisions required.
  • Rathuwadhab FRH: Two rooms in a scenic location, self-catered stay.
  • Mundiyapani FRH: Two rooms, ideal for solitude and deep nature experience.
  • Morghatti & Pakhro FRHs: Peripheral FRHs, two rooms each, great for exploring local wildlife.
